samba服务不连接打印机错误信息关闭

centos 6.2没有打印机，系统日志有大量samba信息：

Apr  8 03:45:56 smbd[14866]: [2012/04/08 03:45:56.734889,  0] printing/print_cups.c:109(cups_connect)

Apr&nbsp; 8 03:45:56 smbd[14866]:&nbsp;&nbsp; Unable to connect to CUPS server localhost:631 - Connection refused

Apr&nbsp; 8 03:45:56 smbd[1787]: [2012/04/08 03:45:56.735377,&nbsp; 0] printing/print_cups.c:468(cups_async_callback)

Apr&nbsp; 8 03:45:56 smbd[1787]:&nbsp;&nbsp; failed to retrieve printer list: NT_STATUS_UNSUCCESSFUL

Apr&nbsp; 8 03:45:56 smbd[1787]: [2012/04/08 03:45:56.736338,&nbsp; 0] smbd/server.c:281(remove_child_pid)

Apr&nbsp; 8 03:45:56 smbd[1787]:&nbsp;&nbsp; Could not find child 14866 -- ignoring

关闭方法，修改/etc/samba/smb.conf

printing = bsd

printcap name = /dev/null

或者

;&nbsp;&nbsp; load printers = yes

load printers = no

;&nbsp;&nbsp; printing = cups

printing = bsd
